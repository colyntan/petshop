Our Mission

We Started as Pet Parents, Just Like You

PawHaven was founded in 2021 after our founder, Jamie, spent hours frustrated trying to find high-quality, affordable pet supplies online — only to receive products that were cheaply made, arrived late, or weren't what was advertised.

So Jamie did what any determined pet parent would do: built the store that should have existed all along. Every product in our catalog is hand-curated, tested in real homes with real pets, and reviewed by licensed veterinarians before it ever reaches your door.

Today, PawHaven serves over 50,000 families across the US and we're just getting started. Our promise is simple: if we wouldn't give it to our own pets, we won't sell it to yours.
